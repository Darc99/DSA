Arrays
Use when you need order

Insertion - it depends (because of re-indexing)
Removal - it depends (because of re-indexing)
Searching - O(N) 
Access - O(1)

push and pop always faster than shift and unshift


Array Methods
push - O(1)
pop - O(1)
shift - O(N)
unshift - O(N)
concat - O(N)
slice - O(N)
splice - O(N)
sort - O(N*log N)
forEach/map/filter/reduce/etc - O(N)
